    CHANDIGARH

  • The capital of Punjab is Chandigarh. It is a union territory and also serves as the capital of the neighboring state of Haryana. Chandigarh was designed by Swiss-French architect Le Corbusier and is known for its modern architecture and urban planning. Some facts about Chandigarh: It was founded in 1952 after India gained independence from British rule. The city is located at the foothills of the Shivalik mountain range in northern India. Chandigarh is the first planned city in India, with its design based on the principles of modern architecture and urban planning. The city is divided into sectors, each with its own market, residential areas, and green spaces. The famous Rock Garden, a sculpture garden made from recycled materials, is located in Chandigarh. The Punjab and Haryana High Court is also located in Chandigarh. The city has a high standard of living and is considered one of the cleanest cities in India. Punjabi, Hindi, and English are the most commonly spoken languages in Chandigarh. Overall, Chandigarh is an important cultural, economic, and political center in northern India, and its modern urban planning and architecture make it a unique destination for visitors.
